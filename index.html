Hyperlink Types Demonstration

Internal Links

Internal links connect different pages or sections within the same website. For example: Jump to External Links Section.

External Links

External links navigate to content outside the current website. For instance: Visit Wikipedia.

Anchor Links

Anchor links navigate within the same page. Click here to go back to the Internal Links section.

Product Links

Product links lead to detailed product pages. For example: Amazon Echo.

Category Links

Category links direct users to grouped sections. For example: Books on Amazon.

Relative Links

Relative links navigate within the same website structure. For example: About Us page (assumes you have an `about.html` file).

Absolute Links

Absolute links use full URLs to reference content. For example: FreeCodeCamp.

Download Links

Download links allow users to get files. For example: Download a Sample PDF.

News Article Links

News article links navigate to individual stories. For example: CNN News.

Video Links

Video links embed or link to video content. For example: Watch a Video on YouTube.

Email Links

Email links allow direct communication. Click to email us: info@example.com.
